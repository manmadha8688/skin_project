What are Skin Diseases?

Skin diseases refer to various conditions affecting the skin, hair, and nails, often causing rashes, inflammation, itchiness, or other visible changes. These conditions can result from genetic factors, infections, allergies, or environmental exposures. Treatment options may include medications, topical creams, or lifestyle adjustments.
Skin diseases can range from mild irritations to chronic conditions, impacting both appearance and health. Early diagnosis and proper care are key to effective treatment and management.
